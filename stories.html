It Took My Brother

Download

When I was nine years old, my brother and I used to play a lot together. But ever since that THING took him, I have felt lonely.

My brother's name was Michael. I am now seventeen years old and writing this as of April 9th, 2018. One day, my brother and

I were playing hide and seek in the woods that surrounded our house. At the beginning of the game, his mission was to find

me; he found me pretty easily. But when round two started, I was supposed to find him. As I counted to ten, there was a

never-ending chill going down my spine. Once I finally got to ten, I went out to find him.


After ten minutes of not finding him, I started to get worried. I went searching for him for another ten minutes, but ended up

empty-handed. As I went to exit the woods, I heard leaves and branches crackle behind me. I turned around in a fast motion

to see who or what it was. I yelled out his name. Nothing. I yelled one more time. Still nothing. I was getting worried

at this point.


"This isn't a game anymore! Come out now!" I yelled.


I turned back around. There it is; more crackling. I turned back around and looked around. To my right, I could see

a dark figure with red eyes and a gray colored mouth that was grinning. I screamed and ran back to the house as fast

as possible. I told my mom what happened and she ended up calling the police. They found nothing. No sign of my

brother or that dark figure. Nothing at all.
